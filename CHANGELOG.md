## 0.1.0

* Initial release. Full driver.js feature parity:
  * Single-element `highlight()` and multi-step `drive()` tours, with
    Next/Previous/Close navigation, keyboard control, click-to-advance, and
    animated stage transitions.
  * Themeable popovers with configurable buttons, progress text, and
    per-step/config/theme render hooks.
  * Pulsing hint beacons (`hints()`) with show/hide/dismiss/restore and
    optional overlay dimming.
* Runs on Flutter web, mobile, and desktop.
* Zero runtime dependencies beyond the Flutter SDK.
